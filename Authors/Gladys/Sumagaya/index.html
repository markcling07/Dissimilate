lumot x sumagaya traverse

Two massive peaks, one rugged traverse, and the mystical mossy forest of Mindanao — a climb of silence, sweat, and wonder.

"In the heart of the mossy forest, the mountain doesn't speak. It simply makes you listen."

Mud & Poses

The traverse tested our limits—scrambling over wet roots, balancing on fallen logs, and sinking into the deep mud of the trail. But the fatigue was always broken by laughter. We paused to balance on logs, pose outside our tents, and look up to enjoy the blue sky breaking through the branches.

Lumot Summit

At last, we emerged on the ridges of Mt. Lumot. Reaching Kalanawan Peak at 2,320 MASL, we stood next to the summit signboard, surrounded by the mossy canopy. It was a moment of pure relief and quiet victory—the traverse complete, and our hearts full of the wild wilderness.
